Roti

A staple of any indian meal.

Roti is a round flatbread native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is made from whole weat flour, and is a integral part of Indian cuisine.

Ingredients:

  1. Whole weat flour
  2. Water
  3. Salt

Recipe Instructions:

  1. Combine salt flour and water to make a soft dough.
  2. Make the dough into medium sized balls.
  3. Flatten the balls using a roller.
  4. Put the flattened dough onto a tava until fully cooked on both sides.
