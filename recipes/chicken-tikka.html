Chicken Tikka

A delicious indian appetizer!

Chunks of chicken marinated in a spieced yoghurt and grilled to perfection

Ingredients:

  1. Chicken
  2. Yoghurt
  3. Chilli Pepper
  4. Spices
  5. Garlic
  6. Ginger Paste
  7. Lemon Juice
  8. Bell Peppers
  9. Onions
Recipe Instructions:
  1. Marinate the chicken and mix everything together.
  2. Cover the chicken and rest it in the fridge for atleast 8 hours.
  3. When you are about to cook the chicken, add diced bell peppers and onions and mix well.
  4. Thread the bell peppers onions and chicken cubes on a skewer.
  5. Place them in a tray and bake the skewers in an oven.
