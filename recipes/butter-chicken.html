Butter Chicken

The ultimate comfort food!

Flavor packed, creamy and delicious.

Serve with butter naan, roti, paratha or basmati rice.

Ingredients:

  1. Chicken
  2. Butter
  3. Spices
  4. Chilli
  5. Pepper
  6. Yoghurt
  7. Tomato
  8. Garlic
  9. Garama Masala
  10. Cashew Nuts

Recipe Instructions :

  1. Marinate the chicken and refrigerate.
  2. Make the punjabi garam masala or decide on a good garam masala to use.
  3. Puree the tomatoes and cashews in a blender until smooth. Filter through a colander to remove the skin and seeds.
  4. The color of your gravy might differ based on the chili powder you use.
  5. Grill the chicken on a stove top, add one teaspoon of butter and burn on high flame.
  6. Cook the chicken till it is just done and all the moisture evaporated. Set the chicken aside.
  7. Make the gravy in a pan by adding your spices, tomato puree, sugar and salt.
  8. Mix and cook till the gravy becomes thick.
  9. Add the chicken to the mix and let it simer till the chicken becomes tender.
